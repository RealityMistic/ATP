This is an application aimed at improving the English listening skills of UNED, public high schools and "Escuelas Oficiales de Idiomas" students.
It has been created as a result of a collaboration between Grupo ATLAS (an UNED research group) and ETSISI of the UPM.

Project director: Timothy Read (ATLAS - UNED)
Co-director: Elena Bárcena (ATLAS - UNED)
Contents: Juan José Magaña Redondo (UNED - "Máster en TIC para la enseñanza y el tratamiento de lenguas")
Programming: César Gayo Bravo (UPM - "Grado en Ingeniería del Software" student)
